What Changed

Released v1.3.0 of my Elixir Phoenix Guide plugin. This release has one focus: testing.

v1.2.0 consolidated four essential skills that enforce Elixir/Phoenix best practices. But testing was the missing piece — the plugin would guide you through writing LiveView components, Ecto schemas, and file uploads, but when you opened a _test.exs file, you were on your own.

v1.3.0 closes that gap.

The Problem with v1.2.0

The four skills in v1.2.0 were comprehensive within their domains. But testing is a cross-cutting concern — it touches everything. There was no dedicated skill that activated when you sat down to write tests.
